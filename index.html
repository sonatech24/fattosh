Fattoush Salad

Ingredients

  • Parsley
  • Tomatoes
  • Green peppers
  • Cucumbers
  • Green onions
  • Radishes

Method

    Cut the bread into the desired shapes (triangles, squares or strips). Toss with olive oil, salt and pepper and fry on medium heat. Make sure to stir frequently until the bread is lightly golden brown.
